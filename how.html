How We Work

We are a customer-oriented company and our mission is to develop top notch solutions, provide you with exceptional service and the best technology advice.

Offering you complete transparency and confidentiality, we help you choose the right methodology and engagement model for your project, develop realistic project plan and schedule within your budget constraints.

Transparency

At Naviam we always guarantee maximum transparency across all project phases starting from a non-disclosure agreement and ending with a support period. Our workflows, processes and project documentation are open to you at any step. We provide you with visibility through regular meetings, project management tools, and often demonstrations of implemented functionality.

Quality Resources

We carefully pick our engineers to get the best resources with bright talent and strong technical skills. Our team is our biggest asset and we do everything to keep engineering staff motivated, trained and highly effective at what they do. Our team consists of individuals with extensive experience and broad technical expertise who're not only capable of accomplishing technical tasks, but also adapting to your company's unique culture and schedule. As a result we provide you the highest value from every hour of work and yet keep reasonable prices.

Collaboration

Software development is a team game and effective collaboration inside the team and with the customer is very important for the win. To succeed we do close integration with your team, plan ahead our communications, perform team building, tune the team space according to the project needs, practice pair programming, and can do pretty much anything your project requires. Flexibility and responsiveness are our basic customer care principles.

iteration

We prefer agile iterative approach to development as it enables us to deliver solutions faster with shorter time to market. Allowing you to adjust the project on the go, Agile is extremely efficient for building products that are always ready to go into production. Iterative process powered by continuous integration results in higher quality, faster release cycles, reduced costs and improved development culture. However we can adopt any methodology you choose for your project or help you to pick the right methodology serving your project needs.

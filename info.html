Company Info

Naviam is a global IT services provider and we specialize in web application development, IT consulting and outsourcing. Our small but experienced team consists of IT professionals who are passionate about software development and thrive at work. We enjoy building elaborate solutions for complex problems and implementing ready-to-market software products for our customers.

Knowledge of the newest technologies and outstanding technical expertise help us to provide exceptional IT services at a competitive price. We deliver cost-effective solutions tailored to the individual business needs of each client, retaining our customer base and maintaining a very high level of clients' satisfaction.

Naviam History

Naviam is a privately owned company with the sales office in White Plains NY, USA and development center in Minsk, Belarus. We started as a group of independent contractors from Belarus working for the same American customer called SericeChannel.

In 2010 Vitali Hatalski, CEO, have founded a company in Minsk, Belarus to satisfy expanding customer's needs for software development services.

In three years Naviam has grown from 4 to 18 employees and continues to advance and mature. In 2013 a sales office was established in the US making us closer to major customers and facilitating further growth.

Our Services

Our experienced team of software professionals can serve your business needs in a number of different ways. We can perform complete turnkey solution development or help you with outsourcing of one or several stages of software development cycle. Whenever your business requires a new tool or product, we will apply our knowledge and skills to implement an intelligent robust solution at a competitive price.

Web Development

We develop powerful Web applications, sites and portals for business and social-focused projects: enterprise portals, various online services, social networking, e-commerce and much more. We can implement feature-rich Web solutions to support your business goals and improve your online presence. Just let us know your business requirements and we'll walk you through the whole process from technology selection to going live. We use the most advanced Microsoft and Cloud technologies to achieve outstanding productivity, stability and security of Web applications.


Mobile Development

We perform all types of mobile application development including versatile business and m-commerce solutions as well as utility and media apps featuring GPS, integration with social networks, video, photo, and audio. We specialize in mobile development for Android and iOS as well as cross-platform development utilizing HTML5 and Cocoa Touch technologies.

Our history of mobile development includes:

  • Feature-rich Business Solutions
  • Mobile Commerce Solutions
  • Multimedia Applications
  • Entertainment Applications
  • Location-based Tools
  • Secure Communication Tools
  • Utility Applications
  • Barcode/QR code Scanners

Cloud Migration and Management

We possess extensive knowledge in a cloud-powered development and migration of Web applications from traditional data centers into a Cloud. Migration usually starts with analysis of your infrastructure, current processes, and change impact. After that, we implement a proof-of-concept and develop a deployment plan. Once the plan is approved, we set up cloud-specific components and processes, perform verification and validation, and finally deploy your system in the Cloud. In addition to that we offer a complete range of support services including setup, management, monitoring and maintenance of your Cloud environment.
